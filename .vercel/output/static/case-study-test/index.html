Designing a Complex Legal Workflow

A case study exploring how we redesigned the internal legal workflow at Ownright Law, reducing processing time by 60% and improving team satisfaction.

The Problem
Legal teams were spending excessive time on routine document processing, leading to bottlenecks and increased error rates. The existing system lacked clear workflows and proper validation steps.

The Solution
